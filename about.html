The Vision Behind My Science Journey

Our Story

Founded in 2023, My Science Journey started as a small community of science lovers. Today, we are a vibrant platform hosting a plethora of events, talks, and resources aimed at making science accessible and exciting for everyone. Mentorship has significantly influenced personal development for many. To pay it forward and assist others, MSJ was founded. Understanding the value of mentorship and guidance, we recognize that the journey in science is not always straightforward and can be approached in various ways. Thus, MSJ provides diverse perspectives and insights into careers in science. Through our platform, individuals can network, collaborate, and connect with mentors who share similar interests, facilitating their professional growth.

Our Mission

To support scientists through a bi-weekly webinar series that shares personal experiences, practical advice, and guidance, helping them navigate professional and personal challenges. Igniting curiosity, fostering knowledge, and connecting science enthusiasts worldwide through inspiring journeys and shared experiences.

Our Vision

To foster a thriving scientific community where knowledge and experiences are shared, promoting the success and well-being of scientists at all career stages.

Our Values

At MSJ, we are committed to making science accessible and exciting, we provide events, talks, and resources to ignite curiosity. By sharing inspiring journeys, we support scientists' professional growth with practical advice and guidance, recognizing diverse approaches to the scientific journey.
