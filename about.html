The Vision Behind My Science Journey

About Us

My Science Journey is a storytelling and mentorship platform dedicated to amplifying the voices of African scientists and innovators. We believe that every science journey matters and that sharing these stories can inspire, connect, and transform how science is understood and practiced across Africa and the world.

Through powerful personal narratives, we highlight the diverse paths, achievements, and challenges of scientists working in research, industry, and community-driven innovation. Our stories celebrate African excellence in STEM while challenging stereotypes about who a scientist is and what science can look like.

Beyond storytelling, My Science Journey fosters mentorship, networking, and collaboration among scientists at all career stages. Our biweekly webinar series and growing digital community create inclusive spaces where aspiring and established scientists can learn from one another, share ideas, and build meaningful connections. Together, we’re shaping a more visible, connected, and empowered community of African scientists; one story at a time.

Our Mission

To make science accessible, relatable, and reachable by amplifying the voices of African scientists through storytelling, mentorship, and community connection.To make science accessible, relatable, and reachable by amplifying the voices of African scientists through storytelling, mentorship, and community connection.

Through our storytelling platform and biweekly webinar series, My Science Journey challenges stereotypes, celebrates diverse scientific journeys, and empowers scientists across Africa & globally to share their stories, build networks, and lead positive change in their fields and communities.

Our Vision

To cultivate a vibrant and connected community of African scientists who share knowledge and experiences, driving research, innovation, and meaningful change across the continent.

We envision an Africa where every scientist’s story inspires the next generation, and where science is inclusive, celebrated, and rooted in community impact.
