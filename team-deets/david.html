The Advocate-Leader

David Githaiga is a data scientist, bioinformatician, and emerging health systems leader committed to advancing equitable, data-driven healthcare in Africa. He builds evidence-based tools, from predictive models to AI-powered clinical support systems, that turn complex data into actionable insights for stronger public health decision-making.

With experience in research, epidemiology, and health governance, David thrives at the intersection of policy, analytics, and innovation. His leadership has earned fellowships from the World Youth Alliance, the Young African Leadership Initiative, and the University of Nairobi Innovation Fellowship, along with sponsorship to the World Youth Forum in Egypt. Guided by ethics, empathy, and strategic clarity, he champions human-centered, scalable solutions that uphold dignity and amplify equity.

His vision is to build resilient health systems that uplift communities and drive lasting transformation across the continent.

The Innovator

David Githaiga is a solutions-driven innovator who uses data, AI, and systems thinking to solve complex public health challenges. He has developed predictive cancer-risk models, built RAG-powered chatbots and clinical support tools for TB diagnosis, and applied analytics in real governance settings through work with the Council of Governors and as a Volunteer Epidemiologist. His innovation track record has earned him fellowships with the Young African Leadership Initiative, the University of Nairobi Innovation Fellowship, and the World Youth Alliance, affirming his potential as a builder and changemaker.

A critical thinker known for clarity and execution, David transforms complexity into functional, evidence-based tools that strengthen decision-making and improve health outcomes. He thrives at the intersection of analytics and impact, driven by curiosity, measurable results, and a commitment to solutions that scale and empower communities.
