The Global Health Researcher

Lilian is a global health professional working at the intersection of health and gender through the lens of data. She explores how gender shapes individual experiences of health, using data storytelling to illuminate these realities and bridge the gap between evidence and action. Throughout her career, she has partnered with leading global health institutions to drive social change in the Global South, with a special focus on co-creating solutions with young people.

The Evidence Translator

Throughout her journey in science, she has seen firsthand that generating evidence is only half the battle. The other half, the one that sparks change is about connection. It's about translating complex data and the scientific rigor behind it into clear stories that connect with the community and connecting with mentors who can help navigate the often-unclear path of a scientific career.
