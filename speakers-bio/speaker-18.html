Dr Fredrick Mobegi

Biography

Bioinformatics lead, Department of Health, Government of Western Australia, Australia

Youtube link: How to Attract the Right Mentor into Your Life

Dr Fredrick Mobegi is Senior Bioinformatician at the Department of Health, Government of Western Australia. He is a bioinformatician and computational biologist with a background in Biochemistry, and experience and skills in microbial and cancer genomics, immunogenetics, population genetics/genomics, (meta)genomics, microbiomics, and transcriptomics.
Fredrick obtained his PhD in 2016 from Radboud University Medical Centre in the Netherlands, an MSc in Bioinformatics degree from Wageningen University & Research Centre, the Netherlands, and a BSc in Biochemistry from Kenyatta University, Kenya. He has had an extensive research career path spanning over 10 years and across four continents. He has worked in health departments, the pharmaceutical industry, non-profit organizations, and academia, and co-authored a few manuscripts/book chapters in peer-reviewed journals. Fredrick has also worked with sequencing data from all major sequencing platforms and has led genomics/genetics efforts in large national and multinational projects. His interests are integrating and translating genomics tools and technologies for sequencing data analysis into basic applications in healthcare and biotechnology. I can work collaboratively in a multidepartment organisation. Fredrick tweets about current affairs and (sometimes ) science with the handle @mobeginomics.
