Mr. Francis Kalule

Biography

Diagnostic Regional Technical Manager for Sub-Saharan Africa, Zoetis

Youtube Interview link: Interview with Mr Francis Kalule

Francis Kalule is a distinguished biomedical laboratory technologist with over a decade of excellence in human diagnostic healthcare and seven years of expertise in veterinary diagnostics. As a specialist in molecular biology and public health, Francis has amassed substantial research experience in both human and animal health. His dedication to advancing diagnostic tools is driven by a passion for improving disease management, combating antimicrobial resistance, and championing the One Health approach.

In his professional capacity, Francis serves as the Diagnostic Regional Technical Manager for Sub-Saharan Africa at Zoetis, a global leader in animal health. He excels at designing, leading, and implementing a diverse range of sustainable growth and realignment initiatives in partnership with various veterinary diagnostic collaborators. His efforts have significantly enhanced diagnostic networks, improved training for laboratory professionals, and established robust diagnostic infrastructures that support the animal health industry across Sub-Saharan Africa.

Beyond his professional role, Francis is actively involved in various impactful initiatives across the African continent. He serves with the Mentorship Guild of the Tony Elumelu Foundation, guiding aspiring African entrepreneurs towards healthcare business success. He is also a member of the Leaders of Africa Institute Alumni Community and the Biochemistry and Biotechnology Professionals Society of Kenya.

Additionally, he is a sought-after speaker at various international conferences, where he promotes the role of veterinary diagnostics in addressing emerging global health challenges. His presentations have inspired many in the field to adopt innovative diagnostic solutions and collaborative approaches to health management.
