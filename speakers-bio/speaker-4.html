Dr. Richard Mbasu

Biography

Proteomics and automation scientist, Novo Nordisk Research Centre, Oxford, UK

Dr. Richard Mbasu, PhD, is an esteemed proteomics and automation scientist at Novo Nordisk Research Centre, Oxford, where his research is dedicated to studying cardiometabolic diseases with a particular focus on diabetes and obesity. Before his current role, he served as a scientist at UCB Biopharma in Slough, UK, and as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Manchester. Dr. Mbasu holds a PhD from the University of Leicester, where he also completed his master's degree, demonstrating a consistent dedication to advancing his expertise and knowledge in the field. His extensive background in proteomics and automation, coupled with his research experience in cardiometabolic diseases, positions him as a highly impactful and knowledgeable contributor to the scientific community.
