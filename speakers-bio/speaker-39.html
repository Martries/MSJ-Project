Dr. Francis Makokha

Biography

Head of Human Health Research - Mt Kenya University

Youtube link: Dr. Francis Makokha - MKU Human Health head - on Cancer Research and Advocacy in Kenya and Africa

Dr Makokha is a specialist in molecular medicine and works as Head: Human Health Research Programme and Acting Director of the Directorate of Research & Innovation, at Mount Kenya University (Kenya). His research focuses on understanding the molecular and genetic drivers of cancer in the Kenyan population. He is currently leading a breast cancer genomics study in Kenya and working towards replicating similar efforts in prostate, cervical, colorectal, and liver cancers. He also works with other collaborators and partners to improve the uptake of HPV vaccine and cervical cancer screening services using implementation science approaches. He is currently collaborating with faculty and sector partners to improve cancer control programmes among people living with HIV in Kenya. He has worked with Machakos County and AIC Kijabe Hospital to set up a population and hospital-based cancer registries respectively
